About

Kim Taehyung owns a small, self-lead, photographic company based in Korea. He is dedicated to broadcasting the beauties of architecture with high-quality photography. With a reputation built on his commitment and love for photography, he provides clients with the highest standard of capturing art through a lens. With Kim’s highly creative self, unique specialty, and state-of-the-art equipment, his photography company vows to capture the most exquisite images.

Kim Taehyung

Born on December 30, 1995 in Daegu, South Korea, Kim Taehyung is known as one of the youngest and most talented photographers in the world. Kim’s love for photography flourished during his childhood where he was given a camera as a gift. Kim always had a keen interest in architecture and photography, more specifically gothic architecture commonly found in European countries. Kim attended Oxford University where he studied in the photography program. Right after graduation, Kim started his own photography company where he instantly gained fame for his unique pictures. Now, Kim continues to do what he loves most: capturing architecture around the world.
